Unslant is a nutritionist for your news diet.

It’s dead simple: whenever you’re reading a news story, Unslant surfaces contrasting takes from outlets on the other side of the political spectrum, creating a more balanced news diet for you — without any intervention on your part.

400 outlets

Unslant supports 400 American news outlets from across the political spectrum. Support for other countries, including India, is planned.

Elegant design

Unslant is designed to be simple and elegant, surfacing only when there’s a high-quality suggestion available.

Cutting-edge tech

Unslant is built on state-of-the-art AI and natural language processing models that analyze the story you’re reading to come up with a recommendation in seconds.
