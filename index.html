“We find comfort among those who agree with us - growth among those who don’t.”

—Frank A. Clark

By reading only those stories which reinforce our existing beliefs, we back ourselves into echo chambers.

Unslant is a browser extension that aims to break these echo chambers by introducing you to new perspectives and ideas.

It’s dead simple: whenever you’re reading a news article, Unslant finds similar articles written from different perspectives, creating a more balanced news diet for you — without any intervention on your part.

Add To Chrome

Unslant won AngelHack 2019 Delhi, and was conceptualized by Anirudh Chauhan, Yashwardhann Kumar, Kabir Goel, Ashay Srivastava and Aryaman Chandra.
